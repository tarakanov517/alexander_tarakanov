# Alexander Tarakanov

**Associate Professor, HSE University**  
**Research Scientist, AI VK**

---

## About Me

I am an Associate Professor at the Faculty of Computer Science, HSE University, and a Research Scientist at AI VK.

My research lies at the intersection of **Machine Learning**, **Scientific Computing**, and **Applied Mathematics**. I develop mathematically grounded machine learning methods for scientific applications and large-scale AI systems.

My current research interests include:

- Physics-Informed Machine Learning
- Scientific Machine Learning
- Representation Learning
- Spectral and Geometric Deep Learning
- Graph and Sheaf Neural Networks
- Recommender Systems

---

## Research Vision

My long-term goal is to develop machine learning methods that combine strong mathematical foundations with practical impact. I am particularly interested in incorporating physical laws, geometry, and optimization principles into modern AI systems.
